About

This National Science Foundation (NSF) Funded Project with grant number 1825023 began in 2018 and with the goal of informing and motivating students to create accessible software.

The Accessibility Learning Labs (ALL) are designed to be easily adoptable labs that both inform and motivate students about foundational topics in creating software that is accessible to everyone.
